Game Rules

  • First player to reach 10 points wins the match
  • A player scores when the ball passes the opponent without being hit
  • The ball bounces off the court edges and must be hit by the paddles
  • The crowd cheers when a player scores a point
  • Serving alternates after each point

Controls

  • Player A (Blue): W (up) / S (down)
  • Player B (Red): Computer controlled
  • Difficulty: Press 1 (Easy), 2 (Medium), or 3 (Hard)
  • Serve: Spacebar
  • Reset: R key or Reset button
